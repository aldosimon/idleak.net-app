Leaks and Breaches

  • Hacking Forums: Traditional forums, particularly those on the dark web, are hubs for selling and trading breached data. You'll find databases, personal information, and corporate credentials for sale.
  • Telegram Channels: Many cybercriminal groups and Initial Access Brokers (IABs) use public and private Telegram channels to advertise new data leaks and sell access to compromised networks.

    • Stealers

  • Telegram Channels and Dedicated Forums: This is where most stolen "logs" are sold. A single log can contain browser passwords, cookies, credit card information, and cryptocurrency wallet keys. These logs are often sold in bulk, categorized by country or value.
  • Marketplaces: Some marketplaces are entirely dedicated to selling stealer logs, often providing automated search functions so buyers can quickly find logs containing specific data, such as credentials for a particular website.
