About the site

The website tries to aggregate data leaks, ransomware, and data breaches that involve Indonesian entities. The data is collected from various sources, including public databases and dark web monitoring services. The goal is to provide a centralized platform for individuals and organizations to check if their data has been compromised and to raise awareness about cybersecurity threats in Indonesia.
